Sai Universal Nivash (SUN) 

         ​Sai Universal Nivash (SUN) is the name of a home present in old Settlement of Kharagpur, West Bengal, India. In this house Bhagawan Sri Sathya Sai Baba (Swami) lives along-with a family. The family belongs to Dr. M. Harinath Patnaik, who is a gold medalist. Swami very lovingly calls M. Harinath Patnaik, as Bangaru. SUN was the name given to this house by Bhagawan Baba himself. Bhagwan has declared that “He has come to SUN to protect Dharma and guide Bangaru and his family.”

The main reason for active presence of Swami at SUN is the love and selflessness of Bangaru and his family. Bangaru and his family members are highly selfless and extremely service oriented. They are tirelessly serving the society by providing food, clothing, medicine, education to the poor and needy people. All the service actives are solely guided by Swami. The cloth distributions and Narayana sevas are held on the last Sunday of every month. Medical camps and door to door outreach programs are held frequently. Free computer education is given by IIT students to the youngsters, who are in need. The aim is to help the youngsters to get a job. Kharagur is very hot during summer. The temperature of kharagpur goes up to 40 degree Celsius. So, in order to help the working laborers, school going students and other commuters to combat the soring summer heat, water and butter milk service will be held for 3 continuous months in summer. The books, pens, bags, shoes and cloths are distributed to the students of near by schools. Every year Sri Sathya Sai Baba’s birthday is celebrated with grandeur associated with large number of service activities. Pallakki seva, involving the procession of Swami’s pallaki and singing of divine chants is held on first day of every month.
