Sai Universal Nivash (SUN) 

         ​Sai Universal Nivash (SUN) is the name of a home present in old Settlement of Kharagpur, West Bengal, India. In this house Bhagawan Sri Sathya Sai Baba (Swami) lives along-with a family. The family belongs to Dr. M. Harinath Patnaik, who is a gold medalist. Swami very lovingly calls M. Harinath Patnaik, as Bangaru. SUN was the name given to this house by Bhagawan Baba himself. Bhagwan has declared that “He has come to SUN to protect Dharma and guide Bangaru and his family.”

How does Baba communicate :

Swami communicates with the family members by writing into a notepad. There is a Swami room, full of Swami photos and divine thrones. Swami sits on the divine throne in this room and writes on a notepad. M Harinath Patnaik (Bangaru) will communicate with Baba, by talking to the photo in the Swami room. After asking questions, he comes out of the room and closes the door of the Swami room. After some time, the door is opened to see the answers written by the Swami to the questions asked by Bangaru. All the activities at SUN are solely guided by Bhagawan Baba. There is no activity which can happen without the prior permission from Swami. Swami has clearly declared that “He is in SUN only because of his Bangaru and He will do anything for his Bangaru”.

How all it started :

Bangaru was very much disappointed and depressed by the physical departure of Baba in march, 2011. He stopped taking his regular food and put himself on light food (palaharamu). After testing his devotion for 4 months, one day Bhagawan appears in his dream and commands him to take food. Baba assured Bangaru that “He will stay along-with them in their house and they should take care of him”. As per the divine command, they offered to food to Swami and Swami started eating from that day. 24 of August 2011 is the day on which Swami came to their house.

The divine Leela’s at Sai Universal Nivash :

The active presence of Swami at SUN can be expirenced through the numerous divine miracles that are happening everyday. Few divine leela’s are mentioned here. Bhagawan Baba eats the food offered to him three times a day. The food offered to Swami gets physically emptied. Swami also eats the sweets and fruits offered by the devotees visiting SUN. Swami accepts the flowers, letters offered by the devotees. The flowers are offered by the devotees on the padukas. Swami confirms the acceptance of the flowers by moving the flowers from padukas to the divine throne. Few of materializations by Swami at SUN include Golden Ganesha Murthy, Golden Lakshmi Idol, Shiva Lingam, Laddu Krishna, Vibhuti, Coins, Sweets, Camphor, Elachi, Bangles, Ganga Jal, Pen, Chocolates, Coconut, Banana, Medicine, Shirdi Prasad, Puri Jaganatha Swami Prasad and Sri Rama Padukas. When Bangaru is performing pooja, Swami showers vibhuti, kumkum, sandle on him. On many occasions, Swami has also put garland, rose flower on Bangaru. On many occasions, Swami has even talked to Bangaru with his vocal voice.

Love in Action :

The main reason for active presence of Swami at SUN is the love and selflessness of Bangaru and his family. Bangaru and his family members are highly selfless and extremely service oriented. They are tirelessly serving the society by providing food, clothing, medicine, education to the poor and needy people. All the service actives are solely guided by Swami. The cloth distributions and Narayana sevas are held on the last Sunday of every month. Medical camps and door to door outreach programs are held frequently. Free computer education is given by IIT students to the youngsters, who are in need. The aim is to help the youngsters to get a job. Kharagur is very hot during summer. The temperature of kharagpur goes up to 40 degree Celsius. So, in order to help the working laborers, school going students and other commuters to combat the soring summer heat, water and butter milk service will be held for 3 continuous months in summer. The books, pens, bags, shoes and cloths are distributed to the students of near by schools. Every year Sri Sathya Sai Baba’s birthday is celebrated with grandeur associated with large number of service activities. Pallakki seva, involving the procession of Swami’s pallaki and singing of divine chants is held on first day of every month.

Why Did Baba Come to SUN:

Bhagawan has repeatedly declared that He has come to SUN to establish a “big Residential Ashram”. He has cautioned all the devotees to work hard towards this goal. Swami would like to establish a Super specialty Hospital and Medical College to provide free health care to all the poor and needy people. Swami has plans to open educational institutions and provide educare free of cost from KG to PG. Swami has even mentioned about Nitya annadan (i.e. Daily Narayana Seva), Vastra daan (i.e. clothes distribution) for poor and needy people.
